DrivePulse

A cutting-edge Progressive Web App transforming standard smartphones into advanced vehicle telemetry and city infrastructure monitoring devices.

Purpose & Problem Solved

The Problem

  • For Drivers: Most drivers lack awareness of micro-habits (harsh braking, rapid acceleration), leading to poor fuel economy and safety risks.
  • For Cities: Municipalities spend millions on survey vans to map road quality. The process is slow, expensive, and quickly outdated.

The Solution

DrivePulse acts as a personal driving coach scoring trips out of 100 based on physics. Through crowdsourcing edge-computing, it passively scans the environment to build a real-time, zero-cost map of city infrastructure (CityPulse module).

CityPulse Engine Features

Pothole Detection

Monitors vertical Z-axis G-force spikes (>1.5G). It mathematically ignores stationary bumps and passenger handling by cross-referencing GPS speed and calculating 3D magnitude to ignore orientation.

Smart Auto-Healing: Removed automatically if 2 cars pass smoothly.

Road Quality Scoring

Using statistical variance over exact 100m GPS segments. Low variance indicates a smooth road, high mathematical variance flags damaged infrastructure.

Noise Pollution

Taps the microphone to analyze RMS of audio frequencies (dB). Required consecutive loud samples to prevent "sneeze" anomalies, and speed filters ignore highway wind over 50km/h.

Privacy: Audio is never recorded, saved, or uploaded.

Security & Offline Data

No backend. No servers. 100% device privacy. All information lives exclusively in your browser.

Content Security Policy (CSP)

DrivePulse enforces a strict CSP meta tag, restricting script execution and dynamically escaping all HTML entities against XSS attacks. Zero surface area for remote injections.
